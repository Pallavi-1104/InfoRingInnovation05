PLACEMENT
IN INFORING

At Inforing Training Institute, placements are not just an outcome — they are a continuous part of your training journey. From the very first day, students receive structured and personalized guidance to ensure they become truly industry-ready.

Our placement support includes mock interviews, resume building, communication and voice training, technical assessments, aptitude practice, HR mentoring, and performance improvement sessions. We prepare students for both Healthcare and IT job roles.

With 6+ years of excellence and branches in Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Nagpur, Kalburgi and Bidar, Inforing has built strong placement connections across both industries. Our students are working in leading Healthcare companies

We also provide strong placement opportunities in the IT domain, with students placed in top companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Tech Mahindra, Cognizant, Accenture, Capgemini, IBM, HCL and various emerging startups in software development, cloud, and data analytics roles.

Whether it's Healthcare or IT — your successful career begins with Inforing.
